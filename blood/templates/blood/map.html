Why This Matters

Thalassemia is a serious inherited blood disorder. Timely access to specialized hospitals is essential for proper diagnosis, regular transfusions, and long-term care. This tool uses your real-time location to help you identify hospitals near you that may offer Thalassemia-related treatments.

Instructions

  • Allow your browser to access your location when prompted.
  • Hospitals nearby will be shown automatically on the map.
  • Click on a marker to view the hospital name and location.
